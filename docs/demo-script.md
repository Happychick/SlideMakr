# SlideMakr Demo Video Script (~3 minutes)

## Opening (15 sec)
**[Screen: SlideMakr landing page]**

"Creating presentations takes hours. You know what you want to say, but building slides is slow.
SlideMakr creates them in seconds -- from your voice."

---

## 1. Voice Creation (60 sec)
**[Screen: Click mic button, start speaking]**

**Say:** "Create a 4-slide pitch deck about AI in healthcare. Include a title slide, a slide about current challenges with bullet points, a slide with a flowchart showing how AI diagnosis works, and a closing slide with key takeaways."

**[Screen: Show the loading/creation process, then the completed slides appear in the preview iframe]**

"In under 30 seconds, SlideMakr created a complete presentation with styled slides, bullet points, and a flowchart -- all from a single voice command."

**[Screen: Scroll through the slides briefly in the preview]**

---

## 2. Voice Editing (60 sec)
**[Screen: Click "Edit with Voice" button]**

"Now let's edit it by voice. I can talk to SlideMakr like a colleague."

**Say:** "Make the title slide background dark blue and change the title font to white."

**[Screen: Watch changes appear in real-time]**

**Say:** "On slide two, add a bar chart showing AI adoption rates: 2020 at 20%, 2022 at 35%, 2024 at 55%, 2026 at 75%."

**[Screen: Chart appears on the slide]**

**Say:** "Add an image related to healthcare technology on the first slide."

**[Screen: Image appears]**

"Every edit happens in real-time through natural conversation. No menus, no clicking -- just speak."

---

## 3. Features Showcase (30 sec)
**[Screen: Quick montage or narrated walkthrough]**

"SlideMakr supports:
- Flowcharts with automatic layouts
- Data charts -- bar, line, pie, and more
- Image search and placement
- Company brand theming
- Tables and custom shapes
- And full Google Slides output you can keep editing."

---

## 4. Sign In & Share (15 sec)
**[Screen: Click "Sign in with Google", complete OAuth]**

"Sign in with Google and your presentation is automatically shared to your Drive. Open it in Google Slides and keep working."

## Closing (15 sec)
**[Screen: Back to landing page]**

"SlideMakr -- from voice to slides in seconds. Built with Google ADK and Gemini 2.5 Flash."

## Production Notes
- **Total time target:** 2.5-3 minutes
- **Record on deployed app** (not localhost)
- **Use Chrome** for best SpeechRecognition support
- **Speak clearly** and pause between commands during editing
- **Have a quiet environment** for voice capture
- **Upload to YouTube** as unlisted, include link in DevPost
