# Clay Outreach Plan

## Audience

Find 5000 consultants and company slide-makers: strategy consultants, boutique consulting partners, chief of staff roles, corporate strategy teams, investor relations, business operations, and sales enablement leaders.

## Clay Segments

- consultants at firms where slides are the work product
- internal strategy and business operations leaders
- investor relations and fundraising operators
- startup chiefs of staff
- sales enablement teams that create repeat decks

## Enrichment

For each lead, enrich company, role, LinkedIn profile, email, and likely slide use case. Add a security note for company buyers that corporate presentations are processed for the requested task and audio is not stored.

## Outreach

Message offer: one week free, personal demo, and direct feedback loop.

Short pitch:

"I am building SlideMakr, a voice-first way to create and edit slides. The goal is first-shot adherence: if you say put a flowchart on slide 6, slide 6 gets the flowchart. I would love to give you one week free, run a 10-minute demo, and learn whether it saves real slide-making time."

## Trial Flow

1. Send demo link.
2. Offer one week free.
3. Run a demo using their actual slide workflow.
4. Ask for feedback after the first deck.
5. At the end of the week, convert to credits or a team plan.

## Qualification

Prioritize people who mention decks, client presentations, strategy, board materials, sales decks, pitch decks, or executive updates.

## Conversion

Convert when the user has created at least two useful decks or says security is the remaining blocker.
