# Video Demo Plan

## Demo Set

1. web voice creation: speak once, show the brief, show the finished deck.
2. voice editing: open an existing deck and say "add a chart on slide 2."
3. Stripe checkout: hit the credit gate, buy credits, generate again.
4. Google Slides add-on: open the sidebar in Google Slides and apply a voice edit.
5. PowerPoint add-in: show the add-in task pane scaffold and explain the upload/edit flow.
6. feedback editing: paste reviewer comments and apply them to the deck.

## TikTok

Hook: "I made slides with my voice in seconds." Keep it under 30 seconds. Show the spoken prompt and final deck.

## LinkedIn

Hook: "Consultants should not spend the first hour arranging slide boxes." Show first-shot adherence: ask for a specific slide requirement and verify it lands on that slide.

## Recording Rules

- Use real prompts with slide-specific instructions.
- Show the timer.
- Show failures honestly if adherence misses.
- End each video with a one-week free trial CTA.
